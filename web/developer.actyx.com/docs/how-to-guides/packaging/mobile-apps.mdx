When developing on the Actyx low-code platform, the way you package your apps is completely up to you.
Depenging on the use-case, the kind of application and the operating system it will run on eventually, the form factor and the way to get to it may vary.
This guide will walk you through a couple of options you have when packaging apps for desktop usage.
